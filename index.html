The platform

From raw tables to clear answers —
in one place.

A governed semantic foundation, and a team of agents that work on top of it. Every question flows straight through to a finished, trusted answer.

  1. Semantic Model

    Maps your business terms to your database so “revenue,” “active user,” and “churn” mean one thing to everyone.

  2. Natural Language Query

    Ask in plain English; get precise, optimized SQL and an instant answer. Saved templates and verified questions return accurate results.

  3. Report Agent

    Turns any question into a full report — summary plus deep-dive analysis — in minutes, and schedules it to the right inboxes automatically.

  4. Analysis Agent

    Goes beyond the numbers: surfaces trends and anomalies, explains the why through attribution and forecasting, and recommends next steps.

  5. SQL Coding Agent

    A smart SQL workspace for your data team — writes queries from plain English, suggests the next step, and autocompletes as you go.
